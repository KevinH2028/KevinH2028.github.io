  • Summer 2025 Bikepacking Trip

    I started in Brookeville, Ohio on June 24th, and rode 700+ miles over 8 days to reach Washington D.C. on July 1st. My whole trip followed the Great American Rail Trail, utilizing sections of the Ohio to Erie Trail(OH), Erie & Ohio Towpath Trail(OH), Panhandle Trail(WV/PA), Great Alleghany Passage (PA/MD), and the C&O Towpath Trail(MD/DC). I rode through Dayton, Columbus, and Mount Vernon and into Massillon, Ohio before curving southeast and heading towards Steubenville at the eastern border of Ohio. After traveling through a short section of West Virginia, I continued through Pittsburgh and followed the GAP to Cumberland, where the trail turns into the C&O Trail and following the Potomac River all the way into Washington DC.
